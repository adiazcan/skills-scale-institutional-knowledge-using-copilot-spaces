# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines the roles, responsibilities, and interactions among team members at OctoAcme. Understanding these personas helps ensure clear ownership, effective collaboration, and successful project delivery across all our initiatives.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** to understand requirements and acceptance criteria
- Works with **UX Designer** to ensure design implementation feasibility and fidelity
- Partners with **QA Engineer** for test automation and coverage implementation
- Coordinates with **Technical Writer** to ensure technical accuracy in documentation
- Engages with **Release Manager** during deployment planning and execution
- Communicates with **Project Manager** on estimates, blockers, and progress updates

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **UX Designer** on requirements, user stories, and design direction
- Collaborates with **Developers** and **Project Manager** on feature prioritization and scope
- Works with **QA Engineer** to validate acceptance criteria are met
- Receives feedback from **Support Lead** about customer needs and pain points
- Coordinates with **Technical Writer** on feature documentation planning
- Aligns with **Release Manager** on feature release timing and announcements

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Coordinates with **Developers** on estimates, timelines, and addressing blockers
- Works closely with **Product Manager** to balance scope, timeline, and resources
- Partners with **QA Engineer** on quality gates and release criteria
- Collaborates with **Release Manager** to plan and track deployment schedules
- Receives regular updates from **Support Lead** on customer issues affecting priorities
- Facilitates communication between all team members and stakeholders

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers craft user experience flows and interaction patterns to ensure products are intuitive and delightful. They conduct research, create prototypes, and advocate for end-user needs throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Craft user experience flows and interaction patterns
- Conduct usability tests and user research
- Collaborate on design specifications and prototypes
- Advocate for end-user needs throughout the development process
- Create wireframes, mockups, and design systems

### Goals
- Ensure products are intuitive and delightful to use
- Validate designs with user feedback before development
- Maintain design consistency across features

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- User research findings and personas
- Design specs and component documentation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Product Manager** on requirements and user stories
- Collaborates with **Developers** on implementation feasibility and design fidelity
- Participates in reviews with **QA Engineer** to ensure design standards are met
- Partners with **Technical Writer** on user interface documentation
- Provides user insights to **Support Lead** to improve customer experience

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers own test planning, strategy, and execution to ensure high product quality. They define and automate test cases, manage bug reports, and validate that features meet acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Own test planning and strategy for features and releases
- Define and automate test cases (unit, integration, E2E)
- Manage bug reports and regression test cycles
- Ensure Definition of Ready (DoR) and Definition of Done (DoD) criteria are met
- Validate acceptance criteria before marking features complete
- Monitor quality metrics and test coverage

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Increase test automation coverage
- Maintain high product quality standards

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and bug reports
- Quality gates and release readiness assessments
- Automation coverage reports

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Developers** for test automation implementation and coverage
- Works with **Project Manager** on quality gates and release criteria
- Collaborates with **Product Manager** to validate acceptance criteria
- Coordinates with **Release Manager** for deployment verification
- Reviews designs with **UX Designer** to ensure testability

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate release schedules, deployment execution, and version control strategy. They ensure smooth, predictable releases while minimizing deployment risks and maintaining clear documentation.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate release schedules and deployment windows
- Manage branch cutoffs and version control strategy
- Prepare release notes and change documentation
- Oversee deployment execution and verification
- Lead post-mortem analysis for deployment incidents
- Maintain release calendar and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Ensure smooth, predictable releases
- Minimize deployment risks and downtime
- Maintain clear release documentation

### Typical Communication
- Release schedules and deployment plans
- Release notes and announcements
- Post-deployment reports and incident reviews

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Synchronizes with **Developers**, **Project Manager**, and **QA Engineer** to plan and execute releases
- Communicates with stakeholders for key announcements and schedules
- Coordinates rollback procedures when needed
- Works with **Technical Writer** on release notes and change documentation
- Receives deployment readiness confirmation from **QA Engineer**

---

## Support Lead / Customer Advocate

### Role Summary
Support Leads represent customer needs and pain points to the product team. They gather post-release feedback, liaise with support channels, and ensure the customer voice is heard in product decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Represent customer needs and pain points to the product team
- Gather and synthesize post-release feedback
- Liaise with customer support channels and ticketing systems
- Escalate critical bugs or usability concerns to engineering
- Track customer sentiment and satisfaction metrics
- Document common issues and workarounds

### Goals
- Ensure customer voice is heard in product decisions
- Reduce customer friction and support burden
- Close the feedback loop from customers to product team

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
- Critical issue escalations
- Support documentation and FAQs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Provides regular feedback to **Product Manager** and **Project Manager** about customer pain points
- Escalates urgent issues to **Developers** as needed
- Collaborates with **Technical Writer** on support documentation
- Shares user feedback with **UX Designer** to inform design improvements
- Coordinates with **QA Engineer** on bug validation and reproduction

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers maintain user-facing and developer documentation to make products easy to understand and use. They ensure documentation stays current with product changes and follows established standards.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain user-facing and developer documentation
- Ensure change tracking and version control for docs
- Review documentation for clarity, completeness, and accuracy
- Create onboarding guides and tutorials
- Document APIs, integration points, and system architecture
- Maintain style guides and documentation standards

### Goals
- Make products easy to understand and use through great docs
- Ensure documentation stays current with product changes
- Reduce support burden through self-service content

### Typical Communication
- Documentation plans and content reviews
- Release documentation updates
- Style guides and templates

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Developers** on technical accuracy
- Works with **QA Engineer** to understand test scenarios
- Partners with **Product Manager** on feature documentation
- Coordinates with **Support Lead** on customer-facing content
- Works with **Release Manager** on release notes
- Ensures onboarding knowledge is always up-to-date with input from all roles

---

## How Roles Interact as a Cohesive Team

At OctoAcme, successful project delivery depends on seamless collaboration across all roles. While each persona has distinct responsibilities, the team functions best when:

- **Communication flows openly**: Regular sync meetings, shared documentation, and transparent updates keep everyone aligned
- **Ownership is clear but collaborative**: Each role owns specific outcomes while actively supporting others
- **Feedback loops are tight**: Customer insights, quality metrics, and team retrospectives drive continuous improvement
- **Dependencies are managed proactively**: Project Managers and Release Managers help coordinate cross-functional work
- **Quality is everyone's responsibility**: From design to deployment, all roles contribute to delivering excellent products

The personas described in this document represent a complete product delivery team where technical excellence, user experience, quality assurance, and customer advocacy work together to create value.
